1. Download and install git client for your operating system. Instruction found here https://git-scm.com/
2. Go to https://github.com/ and signup to create your own personal account.
3. Login to your personal account and visit your github.com/MSUPIONEERS/bookwriting
4. Then click on Folk
5. Check your repositories and at this stage you might have only 1 respository.
6. Click on the repository and click on Clone or download. 
7. At the end of the URL there is a Copy icon, Click to copy.
8. Then go to command prompt and Change directory to the folder you want to keep your work.
9. In front of that folder type: 
    git clone [paste the url you copied in 7 above after a space after clone] then press enter.
